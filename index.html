Common Conditions

Topics we talk about openly

Awareness is the first step to healing. Here are conditions that affect millions — yet remain unspoken.

😔

Depression

A mood disorder causing persistent sadness and loss of interest, affecting daily life and relationships.

😰

Anxiety

Excessive worry and fear that interferes with everyday activities and physical wellbeing.

🔄

PTSD

Trauma-triggered stress responses that affect memory, mood, and sense of safety.

🌊

Bipolar Disorder

Extreme mood swings ranging from emotional highs (mania) to deep lows (depression).
