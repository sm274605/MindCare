Understanding Mental Health

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. Learning about it is the first step toward breaking the stigma and building healthier communities.

The Basics

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act as we cope with life.

Mental health is important at every stage of life — from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

"Mental health is not a destination, but a process. It's about how you drive, not where you're going."

Mental health is health.

Just like physical health, mental health needs attention, care, and sometimes treatment.
